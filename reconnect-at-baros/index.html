RECONNECT AT BAROS

Experience the essence of island life

Turn the clock back to zero - Baros takes you back to simpler times for you to reconnect with what matters the most: rediscovering the simple pleasures of being one with nature while reconnecting with your loved ones.

Reconnect at Baros offers you experiences that demonstrate simple local techniques such as line fishing, opening a coconut, foraging the luscious gardens for herbs and berries and grill over a rustic barbecue bonfire with coconut husk and dried palm leaves on our sandbank.

 
 

ACTIVITIES

  • Sunset Fishing
  • Ocean-to-Table BBQ Dinner on the Sandbank
  • Traditional Cooking Class
  • Traditional techniques workshop
