yoga

Hatha Yoga forms the traditional root of all yoga systems and extends well beyond being a sophisticated physical exercise system. It integrates ideas of ethics, diet, cleansing and pranayama (breathing exercises) in combination with Raja Yoga meditation and is a system for spiritual development of the yogi.

 

 

What to Expect from the workshop:

  • Practice asanas, breathing & relaxation techniques and build an overall practice that suits your needs
  • Learn to de-stress & practice the art of awareness to adapt a more objective approach to life
  • Understand the philosophy behind the yogic teachings and their application to modern life
  • Enjoy gourmet ayurvedic meals during your retreat, prepared by Chef Diwaker Balodi, Executive Chef from Ananda in the Himalayas.

The package includes

  • Breakfast with organic and local product
  • Gourmet ayurvedic meals prepared by Chef Diwaker Balodi, from Ananda in the Himalayas
  • Orientation to the programme and one-on-one discussions to assess personal health and lifestyle
  • Each day includes 4 hours of workshop sessions ( Day 1 - Day 3) 2 hours in the morning and 2 hours in the evening
  • Complimentary Wifi in room and hotel’s area
  • Free garage parking
  • Entrance to Nira Spa by Pure Altitude
  • Free mountain railways and public transports in the Upper Engadin
